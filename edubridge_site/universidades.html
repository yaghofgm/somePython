Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions from partner universities

EduBridge pays tuition directly to the university on behalf of the student. We work with an income-based financing model where students pay only after graduating and securing employment. Your financial risk is zero—we guarantee that all agreed tuition fees will be paid in full and on time, regardless of the student's situation.

Absolutely not. Your university retains full autonomy over its admission criteria and processes. We simply offer a financing option for international students already approved by you. Our integration is designed to seamlessly fit into your existing processes with minimal administrative hurdles.

There are no fees for universities to become EduBridge partners. In fact, the program is designed to increase your revenue by providing access to qualified international students who otherwise could not enroll due to financial barriers. We generate our revenue through participation in the future success of students, not by charging institutions.

We provide partners with a comprehensive dashboard that tracks key metrics such as increases in international enrollments, geographic diversity, retention and completion rates, and payment compliance. We also conduct quarterly reviews with our dedicated relationship team to ensure our partnerships are meeting the established goals.
